    About Casper

    Hi, my name is Casper Kamp. I'm a Danish intermediate-level chess player. Having only played a single classical event, my national rating is only at 1471, but I hope to push it to 1600 soon and eventually 1800.. perhaps 2000 in my next life. Although it is not much, for education I have a bachelor's degree in American Studies from the University of Southern Denmark. I play chess for a hobby, among other things. This site was merely created to entertain me learning some HTML and CSS. Lichess is a great site for playing chess. Go play a game.
